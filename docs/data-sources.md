# Data Sources

## City Coordinates

`src/data/cities.json` is generated from the GeoNames `cities500` dump, filtered to
the 500 most populous city records with population greater than or equal to
500,000.

GeoNames data is available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 License.

- Source: https://download.geonames.org/export/dump/
